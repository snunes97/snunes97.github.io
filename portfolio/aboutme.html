About Me

Hello!

My name is Simão Nunes. I’m a computer science student and I’ve been studying software and web development since 2012.

I've focused mostly on web development, working with both front-end and back-end. I also have a special interest in video game development and have been learning how to make games using the Unity engine since 2018. As a result of learnig how to make games, I've also learned some basics of 3D modeling.

During my time in university, I've been an active member of the IT students' club (NESTI - Núcleo de Estudantes de Informática). As a regular associate, and later a member of the administration, I helped organize multiple events like the annual technology festival Playnesti, the educational event CodeWeek and multiple competitive gaming tournaments.

In the future, I want to keep developing my skills and learn more and better ways to create websites and applications.
